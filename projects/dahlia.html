Dahlia

2021

Dahlia is a display serif typeface, useful for headlines or short to medium-length texts, released through French type foundry vj-type. The typeface is distinctive of the foundry's love of Art Nouveau and atypical shapes. It comes in 3 weights (regular, medium, bold) and 2 widths, (normal, condensed). Jérémy Schneider's choice of letters endings and curves were inspired by flower stems, burgeons and spines. 93 ligatures were drawn. Each letter is designed so that the transition from one letter to the next remains melodious.

Art direction: Jérémy Schneider, Violaine Orsoni
Design: Jérémy Schneider, Lucie Kenny
Mastering & development: Lucie Kenny, Romain Oudin
Animation: Lucie Kenny
