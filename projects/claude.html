Claude

2019

Claude is a typeface sourced from a deep calligraphic study and inspired by my grandmother, a novelist of strong character. The font exists both in display and text. Claude text was especially designed with a need of clear legibility, fitting for long text purposes and featuring classic shapes of humanistic strokes. Claude is available on request and offers more than 500 latin glyphs.

Design, mastering & development: Lucie Kenny
