Claude

2019

Claude is a typeface sourced from a deep calligraphic study and inspired by my grandmother, a novelist of strong character. The font, designed with a need of clear legibility, fits long text purposes and features classic shapes of humanistic strokes, originating from the nib angle. Claude is available on request and offers more than 500 latin glyphs. Claude display is in progress.

Design, mastering & development: Lucie Kenny
