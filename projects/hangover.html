The Hangover

2016

On November 9th, 2016, millions of Americans thought they were waking up from a nightmare. I documented a protest that took place on that day on Columbus Circle in New York City. These photographs, gathered in an self-edited book, attempt at capturing different voices and showcasing strength from people of all ages.
