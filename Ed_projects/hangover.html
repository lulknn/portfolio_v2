The hangover

Book, 2016-2018

On November 9th, 2016, millions of Americans thought they were waking up from a nightmare. I documented a protest that took place on that day at Columbus Circle in New York City. In 2018, I decided to put together these shots capturing different voices and showcasing strenght from people of all ages in this book.
