Balagan

[typography, 2018]

בלגן, or balagan in hebrew, means mess. This term shaped the whole project, from creating a poem full of upside-down interjections and ponctuation to putting it on paper thanks to the old typographic printers in the school's basement. Typesetting was a thrilling challenge leading to this chaotic composition, featured in the editorial project Sans Plomb 95. Thanks to Julien Gineste, Guillaume Guilpart and Jean-Baptiste Normand for helping out.
