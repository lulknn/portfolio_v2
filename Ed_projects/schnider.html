Albrecht Schnider

[Monography, 2018]

This book presents the work of Swiss-German artist Albrecht Schnider. Too often, text in this kind of publication tends to be overleapt by us readers. The layout has been thought-out as a children's book, using large paragraphs facing images as well as making parallels in colors to create lively patterns, therefore engaging the audience in following the artist's story.
