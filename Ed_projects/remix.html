Remix

2020

Remix is a collection of contributions written by selected speakers invited to the Printemps de la Typographie’s 2018 edition in Paris, an event offering numerous talks dedicated to the type field, whether it concerns designing or thinking. Here is an unselected proposal in collaboration with Laure-Anne Labbé and Tina Payet, exploring the idea of a book within a book. The texts are differentiated by their field of study/posture/tone, and placed on a uniform and invariant space.
