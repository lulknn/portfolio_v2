Debout dans les vagues

stone carving, 2018

Excerpt from a poem by Werner Lambersy, engraved in stone during a one-week workshop at La Factorie, Maison de la Poésie in Normandy. Font designer, stone carver and type design teacher Franck Jalleau supervised the workshop along with teacher Philippe Buschinger and Masters coordinator Raphaël Lefeuvre.
