Strejk

2020

Strejk is the result of my experience of living in Sweden for three months. The hectic european elections context as well as the numerous climate strikes around the world, led by young Swedish activish Greta Thunberg, inspired this font. Strejk has four instances and goes from Extra chill to Extra angry. The text frame uses an arabic option, allowing the software to pick out of 4 to 16 differents variations for each letter, in order to make the line of text fit.
